Lasagna Recipe

Description

Cook 9 lasagna noodles according to the package instructions. Brown 1 lb. of ground beef, then add a 24 oz. jar of spaghetti sauce and 1 cup of water. In a separate bowl, mix 1 1/2 cups of ricotta cheese with 1 beaten egg and 2 tablespoons of chopped fresh parsley. Layer the meat sauce, noodles, ricotta mixture, shredded mozzarella cheese, and grated Parmesan cheese in a 9x13 inch baking dish, then bake at 375°F (190°C) for 25 minutes covered with foil and an additional 25 minutes uncovered.

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb. ground beef
  • 1 jar (24 oz.) spaghetti sauce
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 1/2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F.
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, brown the ground beef over medium heat. Drain off any excess fat.
  4. Add the spaghetti sauce to the skillet with the beef, and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. To assemble the lasagna, spread a thin layer of the meat sauce on the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  6. Let the lasagna cool for a few minutes before serving. Enjoy!
